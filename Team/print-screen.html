Printing a TEAM Screen

Some of the screens with TEAM give you the ability to Print via the Apple AirPrint facility. However most screens to not have a built-in print facility.
  • Press and hold down the Sleep/Wake button at the top right corner of your iPad, and click the Home button.
  • Open the app Photos, and you can see your screenshot already saved in the Camera Roll album.
  • You then can print or email that screenshot from the Photos app
