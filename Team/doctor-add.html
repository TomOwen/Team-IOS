Adding a New Doctor

To add a new doctor simply click on the "Doctors" icon on the bottom TAB bar.




Click on the "+" in the top right of the Tool Bar.



You can then enter the Doctor's name. You also can enter "Other information" such as comments etc. After entering the information click "Save" to add the doctor to the TEAM system.

To delete a doctor,  go to the Doctors screen and "swipe" the doctor's name from right to left which will reveal a "Delete" button for that doctor entry.



Simply click on delete then click on the "+" to add the doctor back into TEAM with another name.
