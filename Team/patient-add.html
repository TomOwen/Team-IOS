All About Patients

The very first screen you will see when you login to TEAM is the "Patients" screen. You can always come back to this initial screen by clicking on the bottom TAB bar icon called "Patients".



You can sort the patients by name, doctor, or study, by clicking on the appropriate button.

The search text entry field will search all of the patients, doctors and studies to find all the patients matching your input.

Examples of search:

Find all the patients assigned to Dr. Oz. You might enter oz.
Find all the patients in study/trial 123678. You might enter 1236.
Find patient #105. You might enter 105.
Find patient Paul. You might enter Paul.

Adding a New Patient

If you have a login that has "Administrative" capabilities, then you will be able to add a new patient by clicking on the "+" button in the top toolbar.



Name:

You can enter the patient name here. If you do not wish to have the full name entered, then you can enter just the patient initials or any description you feel appropriate.

ID#:

The patient ID# is very important and must be a unique number or character (i.e. AD124321, 782314, etc.). Do include any spaces within the ID# as the ID# will be used to name the document and image files for the patient. Document and images files stored on your WEB server are an optional component of TEAM.

Study:

You assign each patient to a study or clinical trial. A chooser will popup allowing you to pick the appropriate study or trial.

Doctor:

You assign each patient to a doctor. A Chooser will popup allowing you to pick the appropriate doctor.

Saving the New Patient

When you are done adding the fields to the patient screen, you must click on the "Save" button in the top right of the Tool Bar. A message will tell you that the "Patient ID#" was properly saved. Once that patient has been saved, the next patient can be entered on the same screen.

Patient Data - Changing Name, Study, or Doctor

From the Patient screen you can select a patient to edit and view patient history and much more from the Patient Data screen. Click on one of the patients to display the Patient Data screen.



You can change the patient Name, Study and Doctor on this screen. Remember to click on the "Save" button at the top right Tool Bar.
