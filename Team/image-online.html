Viewing of Scan Images

Patient scans are normally processsed by your radiologist. During this process the radiologist can "capture" a picture/image of a lesion to be stored for viewing by TEAM. This is an optional feature of TEAM. A staff member could also capture the image if the radiologist has not been assigned this responsibility. This facility can be enabled on individual lesion basis.

Scan images (i.e. jpeg etc.) are captured and stored on your TEAM server. Your radiologist or the TEAM Coordinator would perform a screen capture of each of lesions that they wish to include as displayable on TEAM. Those images would then be uploaded to your TEAM server. Special naming of each of the images is required to associate the image with a patient. The filename is automatically generated for your use on the lesion add/edit screens.



When viewing an image with TEAM, you can zoom in on an area of the image. The Patient, scan date and lesion # are automatically added to the displayed image.

Comparing Two images of the same lesion

A very powerful feature of the TEAM system is the ability to view scan images of a lesion from two different scan dates - side by side.

To view and compare two images first choose "Compare Scans" from the Patient Data screen as follows.



Now you will see two lists of Lesion images for various scan dates. Only lesions that have been captured and stored for viewing will show up in these tables. Also the "Image Online" switch setting in the Lesion Edit screen must have been set to the ON position.



You need to choose both a before and after selection from the tables before you can have TEAM retrieve the images.



You now can zoom in on each of the images for comparison.
