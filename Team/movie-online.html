Viewing of Scan Movies

TEAM has the ability to associate an exported "movie" from a patient scan. The movie is tied directly to one of the lesions in a patients scan. Most current image viewing/processing software have the ability to export a movie that can be played by the TEAM system. Three of the more popular movie formats include mov, mp4, and mpv.

Your TEAM coordinator needs to have worked with your IT department or representative to set up this service via the TEAM -ADMIN App. Movies images are exported and stored on your TEAM server. Your radiologist or the TEAM Coordinator would perform the export of each of the movies that they wish to include as displayable on TEAM. Those movies would then be uploaded to your TEAM server. Special naming of the movies is required to associate the movie with a patient. The filename is automatically generated for your use on the lesion add/edit screens.



When viewing a movie with TEAM, you can pause and zoom in on an area of the image.
