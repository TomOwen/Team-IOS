Patient and Study Graphs

Individual Patient Graph

A full color graph of the patient's response over time will provide both the Doctor and the patient with an excellent overview of the progress made. The graph is available from the "Patient Response" screen with the TEAM system.

Additionally, the patient graph can be accessed via a secure WEB based interface.



Study Patient Graph

The study patient graph is a standard waterfall type graph that depicts each of the patients in a particular study/trial. A popup description of the individual patient will show you the patient response, baseline and current scan dates and length of time in the study/trial.



Study Overall PIE Chart

A quick look at the real-time results of a given study/trial is available in a color PIE chart showing all the patients in the study broken down by the studies response criteria.
